Apple-Cinnamon
Muffins

Soft, warmly spiced muffins studded with fresh apple & crunchy almonds

Ingredients

Method

01
Prep the apples

Peel and cut the apples into small pieces. Set aside.

02
Mix dry ingredients

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, vanilla sugar, and chopped almonds. Stir in the apple pieces until evenly coated.

03
Mix wet ingredients

In a separate bowl, whisk the egg, sugar, vegetable oil, and buttermilk until smooth and combined.

04
Combine & fill

Pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture. Stir just until combined — a few lumps are fine. Do not overmix, or the muffins will turn out dry and tough. Divide the batter evenly into a well-greased muffin pan.

05
Bake

Bake at 175°C (350°F) for 20–25 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the centre comes out clean.

06
Cool & serve

Remove from the oven and let the muffins rest in the pan for 5 minutes before carefully turning them out. They taste best while still warm!

Baker's Notes

Apple variety: Use a tart apple like Granny Smith or Braeburn for the best contrast with the sweet batter.

Extra crunch: Sprinkle a little cinnamon sugar on top of each muffin just before baking.

Don't overmix: A lumpy batter is a good batter — overdoing it develops gluten and makes the muffins tough.
