Brussels Sprout

Method

01
Prep the sprouts

Wash and clean the Brussels sprouts, then cut them in half. Bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil, add the sprouts, then reduce heat to medium and cook for 15 minutes until al dente.

02
Fry the onions

Dice the onions and fry in butter over medium heat until glossy and softened.

03
Build the soup

Drain the Brussels sprouts and add them to the pot with the onions. Pour in the vegetable broth and let everything cook together for 5–8 minutes. Remove from heat.

04
Add cream & blend

Reserve a little sour cream for garnishing, then stir the rest into the soup. Purée until smooth using a hand blender or standing blender.

05
Season & serve

Season with salt, pepper, lemon juice, and a grating of nutmeg. Ladle into bowls, top each with a small dollop of sour cream, and finish with a sprinkle of chopped parsley.

Cook's Notes

Broth choice: Vegetable broth keeps it vegetarian and clean-tasting. Chicken or beef broth adds a heartier depth of flavour.

Lighter option: Swap full-fat sour cream for fat-reduced sour cream or crème fraîche — it still gives a lovely silky texture.

Smooth finish: For an extra-velvety result, pass the puréed soup through a fine sieve before serving.
